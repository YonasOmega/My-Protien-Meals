Sweet Soy Chicken Noodles

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Kicap Manis
  • Egg Noodles
  • Broccoli
  • Egg

Preperation

Step 1

  1. Chop up the chicken with a cutting board and place it aside.

    2. Step 2

  2. Heat the pan and pour in oil(perferably sesami oil).

    3. Step 3

  3. Place all the chicken into the pan and fry it.

    4. Step 4

  4. Chop broccoli and add it to the pan.

    5. Step 5

  5. Cook the noodles as per instruction on the packet then add it to the pan.

    6. Step 6

  6. Then add kicap manis to the pan and give it a good mix.

    7. Step 7

  7. Add a little oil then crack an egg to the pan and scramble it and mix it all together.

    8. Step 8

  8. Finally place the food on a dish and enjoy!

Chicken & Pea Risotto

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garden Peas
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Veg stock
  • Risotto rice

Preperation

Step 1

  1. Place the chicken in a tray, add some oil and seasoning and stick it into a preheated oven at 180 degrees.

    2. Step 2

  2. Place 125 grams of risotto rice into a pan and turn the stove on and add some oil and give it a mix.

    3. Step 3

  3. After a few minutes of the rice heating up, add the veg stock slowly until the rosotto is all cooked.

    4. Step 4

  4. In the meantime, grate some parmesan cheese and place it to the side.

    5. Step 5

  5. Once the rice is done add the peas to the pan and give it a couple minutes to heat through and finally add the parmesan cheese into the pan and give it a stir.

    6. Step 6

  6. Once your chicken is finished, take it out and cut them to smaller pieces.

    7. Step 7

  7. Place the food from the pan into a dish with the chicken and finish with the last bit of parmesan cheese and enjoy!

Creamy Prawn Rigatoni

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • Chili
  • Cream cheese
  • Rigatoni(or any pasta of choice)

Preperation

Step 1

  1. Chop up the garlic and the chili and place them aside.

    2. Step 2

  2. Begin cooking 125 grams of the pasta.

    3. Step 3

  3. Then add some oil to a pan and add your garlic and chili to the pan. Your gonna want a nice sizzle.

    4. Step 4

  4. After a few minutes you can add your prawns to the pan and cook them.

    5. Step 5

  5. Once your pasta is all cooked, drain them and add it to the pan.

    6. Step 6

  6. Turn the heat down and add a nice amount of cream cheese and mix it around.

    7. Step 7

  7. Finally place the food into a dish and add some black pepper.

Salmon & Veg Tray Bake

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salmon
  • Potatoes
  • Asparagus

Preperation

Step 1

  1. Chop up the potatoes to equal size and place them all into a bowl.

    2. Step 2

  2. Then add some oil, salt and pepper to your potatoes. Give it a mix and put it in a tray and into your preheatede oven at 200 degrees.

    3. Step 3

  3. Next we chop up some garlic and place it into a bowl along with a knob of butter. Give it a mix.

    4. Step 4

  4. After 15 minutes take out your potatoes out the oven. Move them to one side of the tray and place your asparagus in as well as your salmon.

    5. Step 5

  5. Place a lump of the garlic butter mix on top of each component of the dish in your tray and return it into the oven and give it a cook depending on the size of your salmon.

    6. Step 6

  6. Once the salmon is all cooked, take it out and place it into a dish and enjoy!
